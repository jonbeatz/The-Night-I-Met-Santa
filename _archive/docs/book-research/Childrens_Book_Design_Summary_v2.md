# Children's Book Design Summary v2 (compact)

> **Absorbed 2026-07-15** — same content as v1; living truth is `BOOK-PRODUCTION-SYSTEM.md` §8b.  
> Lulu color correction: **sRGB** for full color (not CMYK-first).

Technical File Requirements: Export interior files as a single, multi-page PDF with all 32 pages in the correct order. The cover file must include the front, back, and spine, following the platform's specific template. Ensure all images are 300 DPI for clarity. Use **sRGB** color mode for Lulu full color (CMYK may convert unexpectedly). Always include a 0.125” bleed on all sides to avoid white gaps during trimming.

Two-Page Spread Layouts: Design using two-page spreads to ensure visual continuity across the "gutter" (center fold). Keep critical elements away from this center area so they aren't lost in the binding. Remember to apply the 0.125" bleed to the outside edges of your spread, and export to Lulu as **single pages in order** (design as spreads; upload as singles).

General Best Practices & Tips:

Always download the printer's specific cover template after page count + paper are fixed.

Flatten all design layers before exporting to PDF to avoid shifting or errors.

Order a physical proof copy to verify color, text, and binding quality before production.

Choose a "Premium Color" option with a heavier paper weight (70lb–80lb) for a sturdier, heirloom-quality feel.
