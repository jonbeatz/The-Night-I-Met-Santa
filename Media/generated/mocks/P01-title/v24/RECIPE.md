# P01 Title — v24 (scenery CENTER, open TOP + BOTTOM)

**Layout intent:** Cozy fireplace + tree vignette **vertically centered** on cream. Equal open wash **above and below** for title (top) + author/credit (bottom).

**Method:**

1. Pillow composite from **v22**: scenic core scaled ~56% height, centered on cream (equal top/bottom text wash) → `_base-hard-top.png`
2. Gentle smoothstep + mild wave dissolve on the **top** crown into cream (sides/bottom already soft from source)

**Why the hard top appeared:** Centering required cropping through mid-paint, which sheared off the soft vignette crown. Sides/bottom kept their native watercolor bleed; the top became a straight cut. v22’s own top wash is also flatter than its bottom. Fal regenerations kept pinning the scene low or leaving a flat wash line.

**Tradeoff:** Soft top is improved vs raw crop, but not identical to the bottom fringe. For the softest native frame, **v22** (scene lower, text on top) still wins.
