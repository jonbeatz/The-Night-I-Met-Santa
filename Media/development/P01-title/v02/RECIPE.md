## Prompt

Create a SINGLE square children's-book TITLE PAGE painting — quiet tone-setter, NOT a story scene. Image 1 = watercolor/gouache style, deep burgundy walls, warm golden light (style-lock). Centered or slightly upper-center: a simple window (dark wood or soft white painted trim) looking out on a snowy winter night. Outside: deep blue-purple night sky, bright full moon, a few stars, distant snow-covered evergreens, soft falling snow. Inside: deep burgundy wall around the window (match image 1 burgundy). Warm golden light from off-frame gently kisses the window frame and wall. Leave open soft space ABOVE the window for the title and BELOW for copyright — intimate, contained. Atmospheric, soft brushstrokes, not hyper-detailed. Soft feathered watercolor vignette into cream (FRAME ON). Art only — no letters, no title, no copyright, no watermark, no people, no faces. Subtle story hint only: the faintest tiny silhouette of Santa's sleigh and reindeer crossing the bright moon — distant, easy to miss, not dominating.
