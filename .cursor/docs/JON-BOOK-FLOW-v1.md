## Design Notes
- Solo blank watercolor text pages can have a little bit of the right side image bleeding into them — subtle spread design element, not completely isolated
- Page count: 32 pages (0-31) + cover
- S4, S6, S10, and Thank You use the text-page-left / image-page-right pattern
- S1 is the only beat with two separate images (not seamless)
- S12 split into two spreads (S12a and S12b) — blessing + God bless
