## What we're aiming for (one paragraph)

An **heirloom Christmas picture book**: painted like classic **gouache and watercolor** storybooks — soft brushwork, blended wet edges, warm hearth light and cool moonlight in the same room. Deep reds and forest greens, cream paper warmth, muted earths. People look real enough to believe, but the feeling is magical and intimate — like Charles Santore and the best Clement C. Moore editions. **Not** colored-pencil / crayony sketch texture, flat vector, cartoon sticker, anime, or plastic CGI / photo-real skin.

---

## Aesthetic pillars

| Pillar | Push toward | Pull away from |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Medium | **Painted gouache + watercolor** (visible soft brush, blended washes) | **Colored pencil**, crayon, scratchy sketch lines, dry pencil hatching |
| Light | Fireplace amber + cool window moonlight; soft gold halos | Flat office light, neon, HDR glare |
| Color | Crimson, pine green, ivory, walnut, muted gold | Barbie pink, electric blue, purple cyber |
| Form | Believable bodies/faces; slightly heightened wonder | Exaggerated cartoon squash, photo porosity |
| Mood | Cozy, quiet magic, intimate close moments | Loud gag comedy, horror, action anime |

**North-star artists / editions:** Charles Santore · Golden Age holiday storybooks · classic *Night Before Christmas* painted editions

## Tips by website

| Site | Tip |
|------|-----|
| **Midjourney** | Paste master block after the scene. Add `--stylize 120`–`250`, `--v 6` (or current). For spreads try `--ar 2:1` + SPREAD master add-on + `--no … gutter fold crease spine`; for pages `--ar 1:1`. Put “no text” in the prompt. |
| **Leonardo / Flux web** | Use master + negative block (include fake gutter / fold line terms) in the Negative field. Prefer “illustration” / “conceptual” presets over “photo.” |
| **ChatGPT / DALL·E** | Lead with “Illustrate (do not photograph):” then the full prompt. Repeat “no text or letters” and for spreads “seamless continuous scene, no center fold line.” |
| **fal / our `image:fal:page`** | Scene + master. Size 2625². |
| **fal / our `image:fal:spread`** | Scene + master + **SPREAD master add-on** + negatives including gutter/fold. Size 5250×2625. |

**Judging tests:** Keep ones that look like **soft painted gouache** (batch-v2 feeling), warm/cool light mix, and quiet corners for text.  
**Reject:** colored-pencil / crayony grain, flat vector, hard outlines, photo-real pores, or embedded titles.
