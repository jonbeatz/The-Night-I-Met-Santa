# SPREAD-FIRST STORY MAP — Proposed (G2.1)

**Status:** Proposed 2026-07-15 (Jon direction) — **not locked until Jon confirms**  
**Replaces:** G2 mix of singles + 4 “hero” spreads  
**Rule:** Every **story** leaf is half of a **full-bleed two-page spread** (5250×2625).  
**Not spreads:** Title · copyright · dedication · About · Thank You · author / quiet end.

---

## Does this make sense?

**Yes.** Picture books read as open *spreads*, not isolated squares. Matching L/R art + putting poem lines on left and/or right is the right heirloom feel. Combining transcript beats into **emotional units** (not one illo per stanza) is correct — art carries the main point; text carries Jack’s voice.

**Cover PJs:** **LOCKED** — oatmeal/taupe + tiny holly (`cover-pj-beige-v2` → `covers/cover-front.png`). Mid-blue runner-up only.

## Page budget (stay at 32)

| Block | Pages | Form |
|-------|------:|------|
| Front matter | **4** | Singles (title, copyright, dedication, About) |

If 12 feels tight later: bump to **40** (16 spreads + same matter) — only if story needs air. Prefer **32** first.

**Body math check:** 15 poem stanzas → **12 illustrated moments** by combining adjacent beats (see below).

## 12 story spreads — text L / R + art idea

Art = **combined main point**, not every line illustrated. Text can sit in quiet zone on left, right, or both (cloud overlay). Keep faces and important props **out of the gutter**.

| Spread | Pages | Poem (LEFT page) | Poem (RIGHT page) | Art (one continuous scene) |
|-------:|------:|------------------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| **S1 Approach** | 5–6 | Stanza 1 (noise / peek / toys / imps) | Stanza 2 lines 1–2 (nerve → decorated door) *or* hold door for S2 | Hall → glow; peek toward living room |
| **S2 Threshold** | 7–8 | Rest of stanza 2 (entered / shock) | Stanza 3 (calm… sneak up on Santa?) | Doorway + **half-seen** Santa among gifts |
| **S3 Eyes met** | 9–10 | “Jaw dropped… never forget” | Splendor / white hair / suspenders | Face-to-face hero moment |
| **S4 Sit here** | 11–12 | Floor / still / gifts galore | “Sit over here. Have a moment to kill.” | Santa on floor beckoning |
| **S5 Chat** | 13–14 | Thrill / chatted & laughed | Who cares, didn’t matter | Warm two-shot laughter |
| **S6 Cocoa** | 15–16 | Places, people, things / toys–rings | Wool, silk / **cocoa instead of milk** | Storytelling + cocoa mug hero |
| **S7 Proof** | 17–18 | Roof noise / need proof | Where… photo — best bet | Child + camera idea; roof cue |
| **S8 Gone** | 19–20 | Flew out / back in a flash | Hour passed / still without proof | Empty room; child with camera |
| **S9 Search** | 21–22 | Hint / hat / shoe / what to do | Flue empty → **something on the chair** | Search → chimney → chair focus |
| **S10 The note** | 23–24 | Not shoe… he left a note / stare | Tore open / words jumped / mention | Note on chair → opening letter |
| **S11 Wish** | 25–26 | More than cakes… silk / hats | **What he wants is simply a note** | Soft letter / tree glow (no readable text in art) |
| **S12 Blessing** | 27–28 | Eggnog / belt loops | Favor: love Christmas, act like a kid, Savior + **God bless.** | Closing blessing mood |

### Optional compress (if you want more matter pages)

Merge **S5+S6** (chat+cocoa) and **S7+S8** (proof+gone) → **10 spreads** = 20 story pages → frees 4 pages for richer About/Thank You. Only if copy feels cramped on 12.
