## 🎨 Design System

### Alternating Rhythm
This book uses a deliberate visual rhythm to avoid "spread fatigue":

| Pattern | Pages | Beats |
|---|---|---|
| **Full Spread** | Images meet seamlessly in the middle | S2, S3, S5, S7, S8, S11, S12a, S12b |
| **Text Page + Image Page** | Left = text on watercolor paper with minimal art · Right = full image | S4, S6, S10, Thank You |
| **Split Images** | Two separate compositions, same scene | S1, S9 |

### Text Pages Rule
Solo text pages (S4 L, S6 L, S10 L, Thank You L) use watercolor paper background with a subtle hint of the right-side image bleeding in. Not fully isolated — a soft design connection to the facing page. Cormorant Garamond Medium 20/26 tracking +5, centered, dark charcoal #2C2C2C.

### Seamless Spread Rule
When images meet in the middle: one continuous painted scene. No fake fold line. No center spine shadow. Background elements cross the gutter — floor, wall, tree, fireplace. Faces and critical props stay off the center fold.

### Wardrobe Lock
Boy: oatmeal/taupe holly pajamas ONLY. NOT a red coat. NOT a Santa suit. NOT a Santa costume. Match Boy G0 reference.
